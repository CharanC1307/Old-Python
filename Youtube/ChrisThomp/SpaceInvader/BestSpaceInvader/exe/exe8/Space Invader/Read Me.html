Space Invader Instructions

WARNING: NEVER EVER CHANGE THE NAME OR MOVE ANY FOLDER OR FILES.
Press the file Space Inavders Applictaion to run the code and play the game.
You can move your player by using the left and right keys.
To shoot your bullet, you must press the space bar.
Press A and D to change the speed of your bullet.
When you kill an enemy you will get 5 points.
Every second you stay alive you get 1 point.
If you kill all the enemies you win.
Try to get the best highscore.
